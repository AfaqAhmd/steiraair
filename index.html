About Steira Air

Steira Air is a specialized HVAC company specializing in Indoor Air Quality. Steira Air is owned and operated by Charles Raglin, a 20+ year Test & Balance Expert.

Our mission is to create Healthy Indoor Environments through our specialized and unique lines of service. We pride ourselves on our ability to stay ahead of the HVAC industry, producing phenomenal HVAC and Healthy Environment solutions.

Our extensive specialized experience in the HVAC industry and cutting-edge proprietary solutions makes us the go-to over any other company. We are driven by the excitement of proving our process, not by the profits to be made. We are always honored to lend our expertise to help you maintain a healthy environment.
